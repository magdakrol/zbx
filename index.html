About ZBX

ZBX is a fully regulated cryptocurrency exchange registered and operated in Europe, which is built with technical solution from Zillion Bits AG - A Switzerland based world leading exchange solution provider. Zillion Bits’ core solution has already been commercially used on several top-tier exchanges such as ZB.com and EXX.com. The founding team of ZBX has broad and intensive experience in traditional financial service, IT and cryptocurrency sectors. ZBX’s core team is located in Malta, Switzerland, Sweden, China and Singapore.
